Our Story


A Case of the Mondays was founded in 2017 by a group of guys working at a bank that volunteered

to play a few songs at the annual corporate meeting.

It went so well, they decided to stay together to make a thing of it!


As the name implies, our focus is on alt rock and pop rock hits from the 90s,

or as we like to call them.. "Oldies for Millienials"


But we have been known to throw a few others in there for fun,

such as, classic rock or acoustic hits.


So we can't guarantee what you'll hear when you see us,

but we CAN guarantee you'll have a great time.
